About Google

Welcome to our world at Google! We are a passionate team dedicated to shaping the future of technology and making information universally accessible and useful. Our journey began in a dorm room at Stanford University in 1998, where our founders, Larry Page and Sergey Brin, embarked on a mission to organize the world's information and make it accessible to everyone.
Since then, Google has grown into a global technology leader, offering a wide range of products and services that touch the lives of billions of people every day.
Some of our main services include:


  Google Search

  Google Maps

  Gmail

  YouTube
